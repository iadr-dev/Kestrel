# Gemini prompt — Kestrel social banner (1200×630 OG image)

Paste the prompt below into Gemini (use **"Image"** / Imagen mode). Save the result as
`public/banner/og.png` at **1200×630**. If Gemini outputs a square or different ratio,
ask it to "regenerate at a 1200×630 landscape aspect ratio (1.91:1)" or crop to that.

> **Note on text:** AI image models often misspell text. If "Kestrel" or the tagline
> renders wrong, generate it *without* text (prompt variant at the bottom) and overlay
> the wording yourself in Figma/Canva — crisper and on-brand.

---

## Primary prompt (with text)

```
A premium 1200x630 landscape social-media banner for "Kestrel", an AI-powered
Taiwan & US stock-analysis platform. Dark fintech aesthetic on a warm near-black
background (#0d0b09) with a subtle radial glow.

Left side: the wordmark "Kestrel" in a clean modern sans-serif, bright gold
(#ffd83d), with a smaller tagline beneath in soft off-white (#f0e9db):
"AI-Powered Taiwan & US Stock Analysis". A small minimalist kestrel-falcon icon /
silhouette in gold sits next to or above the wordmark.

Right side: an elegant, abstract financial scene — a glowing candlestick chart and
a thin upward trend line. Use Taiwan/Asia market color convention: RED candles for
up (#ff5f4a) and GREEN candles for down (#5ee885). Add faint flowing data-grid lines
and a soft golden bokeh glow to suggest "AI".

Style: sleek, high-end, professional fintech, lots of negative space, sharp focus,
cinematic soft lighting, no clutter, no photoreal people, no logos of other brands.
Aspect ratio 1.91:1 (1200x630).
```

## Brand reference
- Background: `#0d0b09` (warm near-black) · Foreground text: `#f0e9db`
- Signal / accent gold: `#ffd83d`
- Candles (Taiwan convention): up = red `#ff5f4a`, down = green `#5ee885`
- Brand mark: a kestrel (small falcon)
- Tone: premium institutional-grade fintech, calm, lots of negative space
